Interesting Facts About T-Shirts

Historical Significance

T-shirts evolved from 19th-century undergarments. They became popular after being issued as standard underclothing by the U.S. Navy in 1913.

World Record

T-shirts became fashionable as outerwear after Marlon Brando wore one in A Streetcar Named Desire (1951).

Precision Engineering

The earliest known printed T-shirt appeared in Life magazine in 1942, featuring the "Air Corps Gunnery School" logo.
